General Energy Saving Tips

HVAC Optimization

  • Set thermostat 2-3°F higher in summer, lower in winter
  • Clean or replace filters monthly
  • Use programmable thermostats
  • Seal air leaks around windows and doors

Kitchen Appliances

  • Run dishwasher only when full
  • Use microwave for small meals
  • Keep refrigerator at 37-40°F
  • Avoid opening oven door while cooking

Laundry Efficiency

  • Wash clothes in cold water
  • Run full loads only
  • Clean lint trap after each use
  • Air dry when possible

Lighting

  • Switch to LED bulbs
  • Use natural light during day
  • Install motion sensors
  • Turn off lights when leaving rooms
